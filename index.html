Jordan: A Buffer Zone for Many

Welcome to a brief collection of CRP (Collateral Repair Project Amman JO) Refugees and their stories. Refugees in Jordan are integrated into society but with great difficulty and hardship. CRP's mission and purpose lie in raising money for refugee livelihood and quality of life. Refugees arrive on their campus to recieve quality education and care. Refugees find themselves at a buffer zone in Jordan with 5 out of 6 of our interviewed refugees wishing to pursue a life outside. They wish to go to high-level education and be successful doctors, lawyers, and engineers and do not let their unique life experiences take them away from ambition and perseverance. How do they stay hopeful even when forced to be dormant in another land for many years? This brief website presents information about 7 CRP kids and their unique experiences. All indeed have past and present hardships, but don't let them discourage future dreams, hopes, and ambitions.
