Jordan: A Buffer Zone for Many

Welcome to a brief and profound collection of CRP (Colateral Repair Project Amman JO) Refugees and their stories. Refugees in Jordan are integrated into society but with great difficulty and hardship. CRP's mission and purpose lies in the raising of money for refugee livelihood and quality of life. Refugees arrive on their campus to recieve quality education and care
